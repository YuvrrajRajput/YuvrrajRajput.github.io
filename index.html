ADENTRA Inc. Commercial Credit Analysis

A full commercial credit analysis of ADENTRA Inc. (TSX: ADEN), a $2.25B North American distributor of specialty architectural building products. Built to replicate the workflow of a junior analyst in a Big Six Canadian bank commercial credit team.

  • Spread four years of financial statements (FY2022 to FY2025) across income statement, balance sheet and cash flow, with all figures sourced from SEDAR+ annual reports
  • Calculated 10 credit metrics including Debt to EBITDA (3.1x), DSCR (1.20x), Interest Coverage (2.8x) and FCF to Total Debt (25.5%), with traffic light conditional formatting across four fiscal years
  • Identified DSCR deterioration from 4.4x to 1.20x as the primary credit risk, stress tested downside scenarios, and authored a two-page credit memo with five risk assessments and a conditional approval recommendation with three maintenance covenants
